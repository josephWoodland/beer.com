How it Works

Glad you asked. Buy Me a Drink links to all social media platforms, so you can find anyone on social and send them the value of a drink, coffee or even a burger. Loved that AnimalCrossing live stream from your favourite gamer? Say thanks with a pint. Want to keep supporting your favourite YouTube make up artist? Buy them a new make-up brush!

1 Sign Up

Use your existing Facebook, Instagram or Gmail account

2 Find

Find the person you want to thank on Beer.com or create a page to start receiving gifts. We link to social networks so everyone you know and love is in one place.

3 Choose

Choose a gift and leave a message! The message and the value of that gift will be sent to your recipient. Receiving gifts? You’ll receive the value of the gift. If you can’t find who you want, we'll take care of that too!
